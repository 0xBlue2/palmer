#### Reports / Complaints
When a Title IX violation has occurred:
- A complaint is a request to investigate the incident.
- A report just informs the school that something has happened.

You do not have to make a complaint if you don't want to. However, depending upon the severity of the event, the Title IX Coordinator or another official may initiate a complaint. They are [legally required](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-34/part-106/section-106.44#p-106.44(f\)(1\)(vi\)) to inform you beforehand and address possible safety concerns.

The respondent is only informed if a complaint is made. You can receive supportive measures without the respondent knowing anything about it.

##### Where It Applies
Title IX applies to anything happening under the school's educational programs and activities. This includes events that happen off-campus or online.
